Welcome to Shmeckle!

What is Shmeckle?

The Shmeckle token is the official token of the Shmeckle community. Shmeckle is a cryptocurrency that can be used as a store of value, like Bitcoin. As an ASA, it takes full advantage of all the features and benefits of the Algorand blockchain. With its catchy name, Shmeckle helps foster friendly banter among spouses, coworkers, family and friends.

Where can I store Shmeckle?

Shmeckle can be held in the official algorand wallet. Follow the setup guide, then search for and enable the asset (ID: 403538605)

Where can I get Shmeckle?

Join our discord to learn about the latest airdrops. Airdrops are a way you can get some Shmeckle for free to start you off.

Where can I buy Shmeckle?

Shmeckle can currently be bought on Tinyman.

Where can I see Shmeckle's price movement?

You can view Shmeckle's market trends on TinyChart.

How can I get the latest news on the project?

Joining the discord is the best place to learn the latest news on the project and engage with the community.

How can I support the project?

  1. Spread the word! Tell your family, friends and coworkers all about Shmeckle.
  2. Put up some liquidity on Tinyman. Putting up liquidity is a very helpful way of ensuring we have market pairs for any buyers / sellers.
  3. Donate to The Foundation! Funds will be used for airdrop transaction costs, server fees, and other misc costs. Donations can be sent in Algo to the Foundation's wallet.
